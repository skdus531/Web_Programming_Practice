General Information

The Networking Laboratory is an academic unit within the Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU). Our mission is to conduct fundamental research and solid experimental to achieve high capacity, highly reliable, secure network softwarization. The current and past sponsors include government agencies (e.g., NRF, IITP, and ETRI), renowned global companies (e.g., SAMSUNG, LG), and SMEs (e.g., NEXDIGM, CHAHOO). The Networking Laboratory’s designations range from undergraduate honor’s students to postdoctoral research associates and visiting scholars. Our state-of-art researches are actively conducted based on collaborations with researchers not only in Korea but also abroad. Current research areas include mobile network softwarization on embedded systems, soft-switch/router architecture and networking, system modeling and simulations, optical networking, clustering, grid computing, wireless ad-hoc and sensor networks, proxy mobile IPv6, machine-to-machine and software-defined networking.
